Workspace Setup

Once all the necessary materials have been obtained, you can set up the workspace for the robot according to the instructions and dimensions in the diagram below.
  1. Fix the bin 17 cm in front of the robot base.
  2. Fix the reorientation fixture onto the bin according to the dimensions in the diagram.
  3. Mount side camera posts outside the bin according to the dimensions in the diagram.
  4. Fix the 3D-printed side camera mount onto the interior side of the post with the bottom edge of the mount 15cm above the bottom of the bin.
  5. Mount the wrist camera between the flange and gripper of the Franka arm.
